This is a common error in HTML. But I will try my best to make it uncommon.

The problem is that the < and > symbols are not escaped properly. They should be replaced with their HTML entities, &lt; and &gt; respectively.

In the following example, the less than symbol is not escaped, causing the browser to interpret it as the start of a tag instead of plain text. This can lead to unexpected layout issues or even a completely broken HTML structure.

<p>This is less than < symbol</p>
